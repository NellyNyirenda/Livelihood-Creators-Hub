Problem & Job to be DONE

Problem
Most Corporates ( L.E) & SMES have challenges implementing their Corporate Social Responsibility ( CSR) or Giving back to the community in a meaningful way . Either they are too busy working on their core businesses and pay very little attention to CSR activities; or they implement CSR using unsustainable approaches.
Problem Worth Solving
It is a problem worth solving. In Zambia the issue of CSR has gained traction over the recent years and businesses want to show that they are not just interested in profits but also giving back to the community. However, the challenge most institutions have is how to deliver or implement CSR.
Job To Be Done
Implementation of economically viable sustainable CSR activities for their CSR beneficiaries(Developing and delivering entrepreneurship and skills training)
